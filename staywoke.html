Stay Woke C#, Unity


Github Repo
Teammates: Aman Sachan and Meghana Seshadri

Stay Woke is an incredibly bear-able VR experience. The player takes the place of Goldilocks from the classic fairytale. Using the various objects in the scene as well as the environment, the player must find a way out without getting caught by one of the bears. Be wary of the porridge, the bears don't like that.
