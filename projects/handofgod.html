Hand of God Unreal EngineRepo

Trailer

Hand of God is a VR Cooperative Endless Runner.

The runner uses a controller to run along the terrain to collect resources and fend off enemies. The god player plays in VR and uses the VR controllers to create floor tiles to bridge gaps between terrain pieces. The god player is also able to block enemies from attacking the runner and can catch the runner if they fall or miss a jump. The goal is to survive and run as long as possible.

I implemented the procedural terrain system. One of the challenges of this was spawning floor tiles in a confined area so the VR player would move as little as possible and so that the tiles don't spawn on top of each other.

This was completed in CIS568: Game Design Fall 2017 with Aman Sachan and Michael Stauffer.
