Typography Projects

I had five roommates during my Disney College Program. As a roommate gift, I decided to create typography designs of their favorite characters. Each character was created using a minamilist design and quotes/lyrics from their respective movies.

Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin Ride Vehicle

This was one of the projects for my 3D Modeling class. After completing my Disney College Program and having been on Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin, I was inspired to redesign the ride vehicle. I looked to one of my old Buzz toys which had a grey/silver/purple color scheme and used that as the base colors. Creating the curve for the vehicle was probably the hardest part.
