Typography Projects

After we had linked up and decided that we were all going to live together during the Disney College Program, my roommates and I decided to partake in the DCP tradition of roommate gifts.

I decided to go the homemade route and do some typographical designs. My original plan was to draw my roommates favorite characters dueling it out in a Star Wars scene, but I was running out of time.

Each character consists of different quotes and lyrics from their respective films. Stitch is my favorite character and was the first typography I ever did back in 2011/2012. The difference with that design and the newer typographies is that I used the entire script from the movie as well as all the lyrics.

I definitely like the abstract look more.
